Mhari Nyce "Smoke God" is an Atlanta-based artists whose music is fueled by raw depictions of reality. His releases in the past year include 'Smoke God", his debut EP, "The Wake and Bake Experience of Mhari Nyce". His Current 4/20/17 release, "Smoke-A-Danktion - A Smokey Danky Funcktion", featuring Bun B. His sound is Fresh, eclectic and easily relatable to the masses, while maintaining the unadulterated authenticity of individuality in his art.
